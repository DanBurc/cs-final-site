Gameplay

Shadow of war is an action-based role playing game set in an open world type envrionment. The characater throughout all expansions and the main game is played in a third person view.

The combat system in the game could be considered a very good blend of the best parts of combat of games such as the Batman Arkham series and Assassins creed. You can manuveur across buildings and areas in a variety of ways and also encounter nemeses randomly in the open, not just in missions.
